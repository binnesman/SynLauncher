    • Note from Binnes: Check out these new addons by Netrinil recently added to the launcher:
    • Questie: Modified Questie to properly track current world event, better querying for actual completed quests even from before installing the addon, and you can track perk unlock/upgrade requirements progress in your quest tracker.
